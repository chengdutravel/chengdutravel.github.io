Top 10 Date Spots in Chengdu for Couples

  • Home
  • /
  • Travel Blog
  • /
  • Blog Details

Chengdu, the capital of Sichuan Province, is a city that effortlessly blends modernity with tradition. Known for its spicy cuisine, relaxed lifestyle, and adorable giant pandas, it’s also a fantastic destination for couples looking to create unforgettable memories. Whether you’re into romantic strolls, cozy cafés, or adventurous outings, Chengdu has something for every couple. Here are the top 10 date spots in Chengdu that will make your time together extra special.

1. Kuanzhai Alley (宽窄巷子)

A Charming Mix of Old and New

Kuanzhai Alley, or "Wide and Narrow Alley," is one of Chengdu’s most iconic spots. This historic area consists of three parallel alleys—Kuan Xiangzi (Wide Alley), Zhai Xiangzi (Narrow Alley), and Jing Xiangzi (Well Alley)—each offering a unique vibe.

Why It’s Perfect for Couples

  • Romantic Ambiance: The traditional Sichuan-style architecture, lantern-lit streets, and quaint teahouses create a dreamy setting.
  • Foodie Heaven: Share local snacks like dan dan mian (spicy noodles) or zhong dumplings while exploring.
  • Cultural Experience: Watch Sichuan opera performances or try calligraphy together.

2. Jinli Ancient Street (锦里古街)

A Nighttime Fairytale

Jinli Ancient Street is a bustling pedestrian street that comes alive at night with red lanterns, street performers, and endless food stalls.

Couple-Friendly Highlights

  • Hand-in-Hand Strolls: The vibrant atmosphere is perfect for wandering and people-watching.
  • Local Delicacies: Try chuan chuan (skewers) or sweet tanghulu (candied fruit) as a shared treat.
  • Souvenir Shopping: Pick up handmade crafts or love locks as keepsakes.

3. Chengdu Panda Base (成都大熊猫繁育研究基地)

Adorable Panda Encounters

No trip to Chengdu is complete without visiting the Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding.

Why Couples Love It

  • Cuteness Overload: Watching pandas munch on bamboo or play is an instant mood booster.
  • Photo Ops: Snap selfies with the pandas for unforgettable memories.
  • Peaceful Surroundings: The lush greenery makes for a serene escape from the city.

4. Dongjiao Memory (东郊记忆)

Industrial Chic Meets Art

Dongjiao Memory is a trendy creative park built in a former factory, now filled with street art, cafés, and live music venues.

Date Ideas Here

  • Art Appreciation: Explore galleries and murals together.
  • Coffee Dates: Hipster cafés like Maan Coffee offer cozy corners for deep conversations.
  • Live Music: Catch a concert or open-mic night for a fun evening.

5. Qingcheng Mountain (青城山)

A Scenic Escape

Just an hour from Chengdu, Qingcheng Mountain is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its Taoist temples and breathtaking views.

Romantic Activities

  • Hiking Together: The trails range from easy walks to challenging climbs.
  • Temple Visits: Light incense and make a wish for your relationship at ancient temples.
  • Hot Springs: Unwind at the nearby Dujiangyan hot springs afterward.

6. Taikoo Li (远洋太古里)

Luxury and Style

Taikoo Li is Chengdu’s premier shopping and dining district, blending high-end brands with traditional courtyard designs.

Perfect for Couples Who Love

  • Fashion & Shopping: Browse international boutiques or local designer stores.
  • Rooftop Bars: Sip cocktails at The Temple House’s rooftop bar with skyline views.
  • People-Watching: The open-air plazas are great for soaking in the vibe.

7. Anshun Bridge (安顺廊桥)

Riverside Romance

Anshun Bridge, spanning the Jin River, is a picturesque spot, especially at night when the bridge and surrounding buildings are illuminated.

Couple Goals

  • Evening Walks: Stroll along the riverbanks and enjoy the reflections on the water.
  • Boat Rides: Take a short cruise to see the city lights from the water.
  • Dinner Dates: Nearby restaurants like The River House offer romantic dining.

8. Wuhou Shrine (武侯祠)

History and Gardens

This temple complex honors Zhuge Liang, a famous strategist from the Three Kingdoms period, and features beautiful gardens.

Why It’s Great for Dates

  • Quiet Retreat: The red walls and bamboo groves create a peaceful atmosphere.
  • Cultural Bonding: Learn about Chinese history while exploring together.
  • Adjacent Jinli Street: Combine your visit with a trip to Jinli for a full day out.

9. Jiuyanqiao Bar Street (九眼桥酒吧街)

Nightlife and Fun

Jiuyanqiao is Chengdu’s go-to spot for bars, live music, and late-night fun.

Date Night Ideas

  • Bar Hopping: Try spots like Little Bar for indie music or Revolution for cocktails.
  • Dancing: Hit a club if you’re in the mood to dance.
  • Late-Night Eats: Grab malatang (spicy hot pot) from a street vendor afterward.

10. Biluoxuan Teahouse (碧落轩茶坊)

Traditional Tea Time

For a slower-paced date, this elegant teahouse offers a taste of Chengdu’s tea culture.

Why Couples Enjoy It

  • Relaxing Vibes: Sip premium teas like jasmine or pu’er in a tranquil setting.
  • Cultural Experience: Learn tea ceremonies together.
  • Deep Talks: The quiet ambiance is ideal for meaningful conversations.

Chengdu’s diverse date spots ensure that every couple can find something to enjoy, whether it’s adventure, culture, or simply soaking in the city’s unique charm.
