Chengdu’s Most Romantic Spots for Couples

  • Home
  • /
  • Travel Blog
  • /
  • Blog Details

Chengdu, the capital of Sichuan Province, is not just famous for its spicy hotpot and adorable giant pandas. This vibrant city is also a hidden gem for couples seeking romantic getaways. From serene parks to charming teahouses and bustling nightlife, Chengdu offers a perfect blend of tranquility and excitement for lovebirds. Here’s a curated list of the most romantic spots in Chengdu that will make your hearts flutter.

1. Jinli Ancient Street: A Stroll Through Time

Why It’s Romantic

Jinli Ancient Street is a picturesque lane that transports couples back to ancient China. With its red lanterns, traditional architecture, and cobblestone pathways, it’s a dreamy setting for a hand-in-hand walk.

What to Do

  • Share a bowl of sweet tanghulu (candied fruit) as you explore the street.
  • Visit the artisan shops for unique couple souvenirs.
  • Stop by a teahouse for a quiet moment over Sichuan tea.

Best Time to Visit

Evening is magical when the lanterns light up, casting a warm glow over the street.

2. People’s Park: Love in the Heart of the City

Why It’s Romantic

People’s Park is Chengdu’s green oasis, where couples can escape the urban hustle. The park’s peaceful lakes, shaded pathways, and blooming flowers create an idyllic atmosphere.

What to Do

  • Rent a paddleboat on the lake and enjoy a private moment on the water.
  • Visit the famous Matchmaking Corner for a fun glimpse into local dating culture.
  • Sip tea at the century-old Heming Teahouse.

Best Time to Visit

Spring and autumn, when the weather is mild and the park is lush.

3. Anshun Bridge: A Riverside Romance

Why It’s Romantic

Anshun Bridge, spanning the Jin River, is a hotspot for couples, especially at night. The illuminated skyline and gentle river breeze set the mood for romance.

What to Do

  • Take an evening walk along the riverbank.
  • Dine at one of the riverside restaurants with a view.
  • Capture a selfie with the dazzling bridge lights as your backdrop.

Best Time to Visit

After sunset, when the city lights create a mesmerizing reflection on the water.

4. Kuanzhai Alley: A Blend of Past and Present

Why It’s Romantic

Kuanzhai Alley (Wide and Narrow Alley) is a beautifully restored historical area where couples can enjoy Chengdu’s cultural charm. The mix of traditional courtyards and trendy cafes makes it a versatile date spot.

What to Do

  • Explore the boutique shops for handmade crafts.
  • Try local snacks like zhong dumplings or ice jelly.
  • Relax in a courtyard café with a cup of artisan coffee.

Best Time to Visit

Late afternoon, when the crowds thin out and the golden light enhances the alley’s beauty.

5. Qingcheng Mountain: A Secluded Escape

Why It’s Romantic

For couples who love nature, Qingcheng Mountain offers a serene retreat. The misty peaks, ancient temples, and fresh air make it a perfect spot for reconnecting.

What to Do

  • Hike the scenic trails hand in hand.
  • Visit the Taoist temples and make a wish together.
  • Stay overnight in a mountain-side guesthouse for a peaceful getaway.

Best Time to Visit

Early morning or weekdays to avoid crowds.

6. Dongjiao Memory: Artsy and Intimate

Why It’s Romantic

Dongjiao Memory is Chengdu’s creative hub, where industrial charm meets modern art. The laid-back vibe and unique installations make it a great place for artistic couples.

What to Do

  • Browse indie galleries and bookstores.
  • Enjoy a craft beer at a hipster bar.
  • Attend a live music or theater performance.

Best Time to Visit

Weekends, when the area hosts cultural events.

7. Taikoo Li: Chic and Sophisticated

Why It’s Romantic

Taikoo Li is Chengdu’s premier shopping and dining district, blending luxury with traditional Sichuan style. Its open-air plazas and stylish ambiance are perfect for a classy date.

What to Do

  • Splurge on a fine-dining experience.
  • Shop for high-end fashion together.
  • Sip cocktails at a rooftop bar with a city view.

Best Time to Visit

Evenings, when the area is lively but not overwhelming.

8. Jiuyanqiao Bar Street: Nighttime Magic

Why It’s Romantic

Jiuyanqiao Bar Street is Chengdu’s go-to spot for nightlife. The neon lights, live music, and cozy bars create an electrifying yet intimate atmosphere.

What to Do

  • Dance the night away at a jazz club.
  • Share a cocktail at a speakeasy-style bar.
  • Walk along the river after midnight for a quiet moment.

Best Time to Visit

Friday or Saturday nights for the best energy.

9. Chengdu Panda Base: Cute and Memorable

Why It’s Romantic

What’s more heartwarming than watching giant pandas munch on bamboo together? The Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding is a unique and adorable date idea.

What to Do

  • Take a couple’s photo with a panda backdrop.
  • Adopt a panda together (symbolically).
  • Stroll through the bamboo forests hand in hand.

Best Time to Visit

Morning, when the pandas are most active.

10. Luodai Ancient Town: A Fairytale Setting

Why It’s Romantic

Luodai Ancient Town, with its Hakka culture and charming streets, feels like stepping into a storybook. The slower pace and historic vibe make it ideal for a relaxed day trip.

What to Do

  • Try Hakka-style smoked duck.
  • Walk across the ancient stone bridges.
  • Visit the small temples and make a wish for your relationship.

Best Time to Visit

Weekdays to avoid tourist crowds.

Whether you’re into nature, history, or urban charm, Chengdu has something for every couple. Pack your bags, grab your partner’s hand, and let the romance unfold in this enchanting city.
